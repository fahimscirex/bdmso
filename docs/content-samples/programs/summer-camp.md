## About this program

The Summer Camp is a multi-day residential program for selected students,
combining intensive Math and Science training with mentorship. Because places
and fees vary by cohort, registration is handled on enquiry rather than online
checkout.

## How to join

- Contact the BdMSO team to express interest
- We'll share dates, fees, and selection details
