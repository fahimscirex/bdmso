## About this program

The BdMSO National Olympiad is the official qualifying exam that selects primary
school students to represent Bangladesh at the International Mathematics and
Science Olympiad (IMSO). It is a 60-minute paper per subject for students in
Class 6 or below.

## What you'll do

- Sit a 60-minute paper in your chosen subject (Mathematics, Science, or both)
- Answer MCQ, short, and broad questions worth 50 marks per subject

## What's next

Top performers advance to the National Residential Camp and the Team Selection
Test (TST).
