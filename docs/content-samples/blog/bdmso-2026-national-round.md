## A record turnout

The 2026 National Round brought together hundreds of primary students across
centres nationwide for the Mathematics and Science papers. The mood was equal
parts nerves and excitement as students filed into the halls.

## What's next

Top scorers advance to the National Residential Camp and the Team Selection
Test, the final step before the team that represents Bangladesh at IMSO is
chosen.
