## About this program

Unplug and connect with the natural world! The SPSB Nature Camp is an exhilarating 4-day residential program focused on outdoor learning and environmental science.

## What you'll do

- Day 1 - Basecamp Basics: arrival, orientation, tent setup and ice-breaker activities
- Day 2 - Flora, Fauna & Ecology: guided forest treks, plant identification and bird watching
- Day 3 - Astronomy & Survival: wilderness survival basics, campfires and guided stargazing sessions
- Day 4 - Conservation Action: team projects on local environmental issues, awards and departure
