## About this program

Give your child the ultimate head start with the BdMSO STEM Foundation Program. Designed for students from Class 3 to 5, this comprehensive 3-month journey introduces young minds to the wonders of Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics.

Rather than rote memorization, the program nurtures natural curiosity through interactive sessions, everyday examples, and foundational problem-solving.

## What you'll do

- Basic Science: Intro to STEM, scientific method, measurement, lab basics
- Chemistry: States of matter, periodic table
- Physics: Newton's laws, energy, thermodynamics, electricity, simple machines
- Math / Data: Coordinate geometry, data & graphs, intro to algebra
- Coding: Intro to Scratch, if/then logic, loops, debugging, game design
- Astronomy: Space, constellations, star formation
- Earth & Environmental Science
- Biology: Ecosystems, human body, first aid
- Robotics: Kids' AI and machine learning
- Laboratory Basics and Experiment Session

## Class Schedule

Every Sunday and Tuesday, 5pm to 7pm at MASLab, Dhanmondi, Dhaka
