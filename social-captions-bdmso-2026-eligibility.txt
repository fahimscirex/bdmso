🔬 BdMSO 2026 — Who can take part?

A lot of parents have been asking us about eligibility for the next cycle, so here's everything in one place.

Your child can participate in BdMSO 2026 if they meet ALL four criteria:

01. Must be 13 years old or younger
02. Born on or after October 1, 2013
03. Enrolled in Grade 6 or lower as of October 1, 2026
04. British curriculum (or similar streams) students who are promoted to Grade 7 in July/August 2026 are NOT eligible — even if they were in Grade 6 during the May registration window

Eligibility date: October 1, 2026

Registration for the next cycle is not open yet — follow our page and we'll announce the moment it does.

Full details, syllabus, and updates → bdmso.org
