BdMSO Quiz Competition

A general-knowledge quiz round for our youngest learners - open to Bangla and English mediums.

About this program

The BdMSO Quiz Competition is an encouragement category for primary-school students up to Class 3 - designed to make their first competitive STEM experience welcoming, fun and curiosity-driven.

Unlike the National Olympiad, the Quiz does not lead to the National Camp or the TST - but it's open to both Bangla and English mediums and is the perfect first step for younger siblings or first-time competitors.

What you'll do

  • Answer general-knowledge questions across Math, Science and curiosity-building topics
  • Compete at the same venue as the National Olympiad on the same day
  • Take home a Participant T-shirt, badge and Certificate of Participation
  • Top scorers receive a separate Quiz Champion certificate
