BdMSO Quiz Competition

National Quiz competition on Mathematics and Science held on the same day as the National Olympiad for the youngest learners.

About this program

A National Quiz Competition on Mathematics and Science held on the same day as the National Olympiad for the youngest learners (Class three or below). It is designed to make their first competitive STEM experience welcoming, fun and curiosity driven.

Quiz Competition does not lead to the camp or International Participation. It is the first step towards a competitive journey for our country's youngest Mathematics and Science enthusiasts.

What You'll Get

  • Certificate of Participation, BdMSO Tshirt, ID Card, Badges, Snacks and Lunch
  • Winners receive Medal and Certificate of Achievement
  • Highest scorer of the Quiz receives a Champion trophy

Program Day Outline

  • Registration
  • Opening Ceremony
  • Exam (Quiz)
  • Lunch Break
  • Cultural Program
  • Prize Giving Ceremony

Quiz Competition Structure

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Format: Mix of MCQ and Short Question
  • Topics: Basic Elementary Mathematics and Science
