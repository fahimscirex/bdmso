About the program

This program provides a foundational intervention for primary students (Grade 5 and below), aiming to strengthen fundamental mathematics and science skills. Inspired by Maryam Mirzakhani's creative approach to problem-solving, our curriculum builds deep conceptual understanding and a lasting passion for STEM in early-age learners.

Give your child an early, competitive edge with targeted, advanced training — to unlock their full potential and prepare them for a successful journey ahead.
