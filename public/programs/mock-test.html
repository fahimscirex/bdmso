BdMSO Mock Test

Mock exam held on June 12 and June 19 to benchmark your skills and understand your standing. Available for Science, Math, or both subjects.

About this program

Exam confidence comes from practice. The BdMSO Mock Test offers on-site, simulated exams designed to exactly mirror the pressure, time constraints and question patterns of the actual BdMSO.

Hosted at MASLab in Dhaka, these monthly tests help students master their time management and identify weak points before the main event. Detailed leaderboards and results are published after each test.
