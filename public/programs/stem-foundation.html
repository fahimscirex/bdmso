STEM Foundation Program

The ultimate head start in Science, Technology, Engineering and Math - for Class 2 to 8.

About this program

Give your child the ultimate head start with the BdMSO STEM Foundation Program. Designed for students from Class 2 to 8, this comprehensive 24-class journey introduces young minds to the wonders of Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics.

Rather than rote memorization, the program nurtures natural curiosity through interactive sessions, everyday examples, and foundational problem-solving. Students are divided into age-appropriate batches (Class 2–3, 4–6, 7–8) so each cohort moves at the right pace.

What you'll do

  • Basic Science (Class 1–2): Intro to STEM, scientific method, measurement, lab basics
  • Chemistry (Class 3): States of matter, periodic table
  • Physics (Class 4–6): Newton's laws, energy, thermodynamics, electricity, simple machines
  • Math / Data (Class 7, 14): Coordinate geometry, data & graphs, intro to algebra
  • Coding (Class 8–9): Intro to Scratch, if/then logic, loops, debugging, game design
  • Astronomy (Class 10): Space, constellations, star formation
  • Earth & Environmental Science (Class 11–12)
  • Biology (Class 13, 15, 17): Ecosystems, human body, first aid
  • Genetics (Class 16): Introduction to DNA
  • Robotics (Class 18): Kids' AI and machine learning
  • Class 19: Online review session
  • Class 20: MASLab Experience (offline lab visit)
