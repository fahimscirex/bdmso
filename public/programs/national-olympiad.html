BdMSO National Olympiad

Your child's path to representing Bangladesh at IMSO.

About this program

The BdMSO National Olympiad is the official Math + Science Olympiad - 60 minutes per subject, covering Mathematics and Science (50 + 50 = 100 marks). It is the only path into the National Residential Camp and, ultimately, Bangladesh's delegation to the International Mathematics & Science Olympiad (IMSO).

The exam runs at multiple regional venues across Bangladesh. Each program includes the National Anthem, the exam itself, a lunch break, a cultural program, and a prize-giving ceremony - so it's both an evaluation and a celebration of primary-school STEM.

What you'll do

  • Sit a 60-minute exam per subject - Mathematics and Science (50 + 50 = 100 marks)
  • Compete at your nearest regional venue (Dhaka, Chattogram and more)
  • Take home a Participant T-shirt, ID, badge, sticker and Certificate of Participation
  • If you place in the top ~20–30%, you're designated a Winner and invited to the National Residential Camp

After the Olympiad

  • Top ~20–30% of participants → Winners (medal + Certificate of Achievement)
  • Highest scorer in each section → Champion Trophy (Mathematics, Science, Combined)
  • Every contestant → Certificate of Participation
  • Winners invited to the 3-day National Residential Camp
  • Camp graduates take the Team Selection Test (TST); top scorers form Bangladesh's IMSO delegation
