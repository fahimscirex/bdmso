BdMSO National Olympiad

Your child's path to representing Bangladesh at IMSO.

About this program

BdMSO National Olympiad will be held in multiple regional venues, simultaneously on the same date. The assessment is a two-hour written exam combining both Mathematics and Science sections, featuring a mix of Multiple-Choice Questions (MCQ), Short, and Broad Questions. See Mark Distribution Here.

This competition day typically commences with the National Anthem, followed by the exam, a lunch break, a cultural program, and a prize giving ceremony.

Invitation letters for the National Residential Camp are handed over to the guardians of medalists immediately following the ceremony on the same day.

What You'll Get

  • All Participants Receive a Certificate of Participation, A BdMSO Tshirt, ID Card, Badges, Snacks and Lunch.
  • Winners will receive Certificate of Achievement and Winners Medal
  • Winners Category: Mathematics Winner, Science Winner and All-Round Winners
  • The highest scorer of the National Olympiad on each subject combining will receive a Champions trophy. This will be awarded on the residential camp.
  • All winners will be invited to the national residential camp
  • Camp graduates will take part in the Team Selection Test (TST)
  • Top scorers from TST will be the part of Bangladesh's Delegations for 23rd IMSO, in Indonesia this October.
