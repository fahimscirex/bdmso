About this program

Take your Olympiad journey to the next level with the BdMSO Preparatory Course. This intensive 12-class program is meticulously crafted for students in Class 3 to 5 who are aiming to compete at the national level.

The structured training focuses heavily on advanced problem-solving, critical thinking, and Olympiad-style question patterns. The course also includes 2 mock tests (12 June and 19 June 2026) to evaluate readiness.

What you'll do

  • Class 1: Scientific thinking, measurements, experimental design
  • Class 2: Basics of life, plant science, health and metabolism
  • Class 3: Human anatomy and organ system - Part 1
  • Class 4: Human anatomy and organ system - Part 2
  • Class 5: Chemistry fundamentals, matter and fluids
  • Class 6: Forces and motion, energy and thermal properties
  • Class 7: Light and sound, electricity and magnetism
  • Class 8: Earth system, solar system, natural calamities
  • Class 9: Global geography, geography and Bangladesh
  • Class 10: Ecology and animal behaviour, environmental issues, conservation action
  • Class 11: Microbiology and genetics, biotechnology and modern science
  • Class 12: Review & final assessments
  • Math outline: announced soon
  • Mock Test 1: 12 June 2026 · Mock Test 2: 19 June 2026
