About this program

Take your Olympiad journey to the next level with the BdMSO Preparatory Course. This basic-level online program is meticulously crafted for students in Class 6 or below who are aiming to compete at the national level.

The structured training focuses on problem-solving, critical thinking, and Olympiad-style question patterns over 10-sessions of live online classes. The course also includes 2 mock tests (12 June and 19 June 2026) to evaluate readiness. Held from 6 to 26 June 2026.
