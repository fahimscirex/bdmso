About BdMSO

Bangladesh's gateway to the International Math & Science Olympiad.

Organised by BdOSN and SPSB, BdMSO has sent a national delegation to every IMSO since our founding.

Our Story

Built by educators, run by volunteers, shaped by students.

BdMSO exists to give every curious child in Bangladesh a real pathway to the international stage. We start with a nationwide qualifying round open to every Class 3–6 student — and from there, we train, mentor, and travel together until a small team stands with our country's flag at IMSO.

Along the way, we build year-round programs — preparatory courses, lab workshops, summer camps, and masterclasses — because the journey matters as much as the destination.

What we stand for

Three commitments that shape everything we do.

Access, not gatekeeping

The National Round is open to every eligible student in Bangladesh — curriculum, school type, and location regardless.

Fairness & privacy

Numerical scores stay private. We celebrate medals alphabetically and protect student privacy at every stage.

Year-round learning

The olympiad is one week a year. The habits of curiosity and problem-solving are for life.

Organising Bodies

BdOSN & SPSB.

BdOSN

Bangladesh Open Source Network

BdOSN champions open knowledge, computing, and science education across Bangladesh — from the National Digital Innovation Awards to nationwide olympiads.

SPSB

Society for the Popularization of Science, Bangladesh

SPSB has been making science accessible to Bangladeshi students since its founding — through summer camps, publications, and research mentorship.
