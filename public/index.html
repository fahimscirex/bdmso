About BdMSO

Bangladesh's gateway to the International Math & Science Olympiad.

BdMSO is the official qualifying platform for IMSO 2026 in Indonesia. Jointly organised by BdOSN and SPSB, we nurture primary school students from Class 3 to 6 through rigorous training, mentorship, and residential camps that prepare them to represent Bangladesh on the world stage.

From a single qualifying round to the final delegation, every step is designed to build confidence, curiosity, and a lifelong love for science and mathematics.
